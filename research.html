Applied research with IMPACT Inititiatives:

1. Community-based monitoring of the STREAM Programme project

I have supervised the CBM project for the STREAM Programme, aimed to hand over ongoing monitoring capacity to beneficiaries in communities of intervention to improve project accountability, and identify additional areas where support is needed and highlight emergent needs. Thus, the purpose is to set up mutual accountability between communities and programme implementors in the selected communities of Kyrgyzstan, Tajikistan and Uzbekistan.
Objectives:
1. Monitor the overall progress of the programme and ensure that activities align with the expected outcomes and indicators.
2. Improve information management skills, such as data collection and data analysis, of beneficiaries through targeted capacity-building initiatives on community-based [project implementation]community-based monitoring.
3. Identify and monitor emerging environmental issues and needs (mitigation measures) of the communities of intervention, enabling timely interventions and adjustments within the project's framework.
4. Assess how program activities address the specific needs and priorities of women, men, and marginalized groups in target communities.
